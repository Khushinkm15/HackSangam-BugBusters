Empowering Life-Saving Efforts

"AERO (Aviation Emergency Rescue Optimizer) stands as the forefront solution in response decision support systems tailored for aviation search and rescue missions. Representing the latest evolution in technology, AERO version 4 embodies two critical components: Aircraft Crash Location Determination (ACLD) and Rescue Operation Coordination (ROC), aligning seamlessly with established international standards such as those outlined in the ICAO SARPs."

Problem

Loss of aircraft contact often results in tragic loss of life. However, pinpointing the crash location accurately, even in rare instances, significantly increases the chances of successful rescue operations and potential survival.

User-Friendly Interface

AERO offers a clear, step-by-step interface for rapid setup during emergencies by all personnel.

Metocean Forecast Integration

AERO seamlessly integrates with metocean forecast data (NetCDF or GRIB formats) for enhanced search accuracy.

Target Database

AERO includes an extensive built-in database of target types, streamlining search objectives matching.

Advanced Planning Tools

AERO provides Monte Carlo, Datum Point, Datum Line, and Backtrack planning features for comprehensive planning.

Charting Support

AERO supports S57 ENC and Shape chart formats, facilitating situational awareness and navigation.

Dynamic Target Visualization

AERO enables chart-based display of target trajectories and metocean conditions for effective search area determination.

Flexible Results Outputs

AERO offers results output options in PDF, RTF, KML, and XML formats for easy data sharing.

Comprehensive Training

Access online training materials and support videos to enhance user proficiency with AERO
